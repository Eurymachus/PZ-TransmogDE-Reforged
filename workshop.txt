description=[hr][/hr]
description=[h2]What it does[/h2]
description=[i]Right-click on an item > Transmog Menu[/i]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Transmog Viewer:[/b] hot-swap the appearance of any piece of clothing or armor.
description=[*][b]Hide/Show:[/b] sets the visibility on an item.
description=[*][b]Colour Picker:[/b] change to a wide range or supported colours.
description=[*][b]Texture Picker:[/b] if the item allows you can switch the texture.
description=[/list]

description=[h2]Sandbox Options[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Immersive Mode:[/b] (Default: [i]Enabled[/i]) You can only Transmog to items you have 'seen' inside containers/ground.
description=[*][b]Limit Body Location:[/b] (Default: [i]Enabled[/i]) Hats can only be Transmogged to other Hats, Pants to other Legwear, Rucksacks to other backpacks etc.
description=[/list]

description=[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Build:[/b] 42.*
description=[*][b]UI mods:[/b] Generally compatible. Mods replacing vanilla Item Tooltips will likely intefere (depending on mod load order).
description=[*][b][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3036830549]Better Clothing Info[/url][/b]
description= - Directly compatible with BCIC tooltips
description=[*][b][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2950902979]Equipment UI[/url][/b]
description= - Directly compatible with the EUI Paperdoll
description=[*][b]Clothing mods:[/b] In theory compatible; the Transmogable items should appear in the List Viewer.
description=[*]Vanilla Tooltip is replaced via hooks; all other hooks are pass-through so fully compatibile.
description=[/list]
